    Comment moderation rules
    ActionWhen to applyMark in Konnect
    Hide / Delete Spam ads, abusive or obscene content, off-topic trolling, community standards violation. "Invalid/Spam" or "Community Guidelines Violation"
    Hide ONLY User accidentally posts personal data (e.g. phone number). DM the user after hiding. Tag as "Hidden" + note why
    Do NOT Delete Honest criticism or legitimate service queries. Respond per Negative Sentiment guidelines. Respond, update status
    Audit note Any hidden/deleted comment — always. Add a KI note explaining why it was removed.
    Reopen rules
    TriggerAction
    Customer replies to a closed ticketReopen (Konnect can auto-reopen). Address concern before re-closing.
    Customer sends a positive / thank-you replyClose the ticket immediately.
    Customer shows dissatisfactionAddress the concern fully, then close. Target: within 24 hours.
    Irrelevant ticket identification
    Spam message Non-Wiom related text Auto-generated emails Immediate reply bots Non-related photos/images
    Mark these as Irrelevant Ticket in Konnect and do not spend time on resolution.
    Escalation matrix
    L1 — Immediate Supervisor / Manager
    Triggers: FRT/TAT targets missed · Unable to collect necessary customer details · High-volume or sentiment spike.
    Action: Immediate supervisor notification + internal mail tag. Notify in Slack channel Cx_all.
    L2 — Director / Head
    Triggers: Major brand crisis · Severe reputation risk · Legal / safety issue · System downtime impacting SLAs.
    Action: Alert L2 immediately via internal tag or email. Use subject line: "Critical".
    Violated SLA — immediate steps
    1. Flag the ticket as overdue in Konnect Insight.
    2. Notify supervisor immediately — do not wait till end of shift.
    3. Add an internal note with reason for delay.
    4. If >30 min without FRT → escalate to L1.
    5. If >48 hrs without resolution → escalate to L2 and document.
    Start of shift
    • 1
      Log into all systems
      KI, WIOM HUB, Ameyo, Slack, Gmail
    • 2
      Review overnight tickets
      Check SLA deadlines — prioritise urgent, escalated, or aging tickets first.
    • 3
      Acknowledge any missed FRTs
      Send pending first responses before anything else.
    End of shift (7:30 pm)
    • 1
      Update all open tickets
      Ensure every open ticket has a current status and notes.
    • 2
      Reset queue
      Use "Reset my queue" in KI to hand off to next shift.
    • 3
      Share EOD report in Slack
      Post summary in Cx_all — tickets received / resolved / escalated + high-priority ongoing issues.
